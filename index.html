About Me

Hey there, net surfer! My name is Steven Bleifer and this is my little corner of the internet.

I'm currently finishing up my Master of Science in Accounting at NYU Stern School of Business (Class of 2026), where I also serve as a Graduate Student Ambassador for the MS in Accounting program. Before that, I graduated Summa Cum Laude from the University of Pittsburgh with a BS in Business Administration.

This fall, I'll be joining KPMG as a Commercial Audit Associate. I've already interned at both KPMG and EY, and I love the world of audit and accounting.

When I'm not crunching numbers, you'll find me playing guitar and bass, doing the NYT crossword, watching my teams, or surfing the web!
