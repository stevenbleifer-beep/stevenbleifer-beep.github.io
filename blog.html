March 2026

Hello, world.

This is the first post on a corner of the internet I've been meaning to build for a while. The plan is simple — write occasionally, keep it honest, and avoid the temptation to overdesign.

A bit of context. I'm finishing my MS in Accounting at NYU Stern, where I'm a Graduate Student Ambassador for the program. This fall I'll start as a Commercial Audit Associate at KPMG in Morristown.

The route here was: Pitt Summa Cum Laude, an EY summer in Pittsburgh, a year teaching financial accounting as a TA, then a KPMG internship in Short Hills last summer. Stern wraps in May.

I want to use this space to write about audit and accounting as I learn them — not as polished thinkpieces, but as honest notes. The kinds of things I wish I'd read when I was choosing between firms, picking electives, or trying to make sense of a new client. I'll also occasionally write about things outside the day job: guitar, photography, records, the New York Times crossword.

If you're reading this, thanks for stopping by. You can leave a note in the guestbook, send me an email, or find me on LinkedIn.

— Steven
