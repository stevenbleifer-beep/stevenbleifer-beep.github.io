Welcome.

This is the first post on a site I've been meaning to build for some time. The intent is straightforward: occasional writing on accounting and audit, paired with a few notes on what I'm learning along the way.

For context — I'm completing my MS in Accounting at NYU Stern, where I serve as a Graduate Student Ambassador. This fall I'll join KPMG as a Commercial Audit Associate in Morristown.

Before Stern: a B.S. in Accounting from the University of Pittsburgh (Summa Cum Laude), a summer with EY in Pittsburgh, a year as a Teaching Assistant for Introduction to Financial Accounting, and a KPMG internship in Short Hills. Stern wraps in May.

Posts here will focus primarily on audit and accounting — practical observations from school and early career. The occasional piece on guitar, photography, or film as well.

Thanks for reading. You can leave a note in the guestbook, reach me by email, or connect on LinkedIn.

— Steven
