Hello World! Welcome to My Site

📅 Posted: March 2026   |   👤 By: Steven

Welcome to my blog! Or as we called it in the 90's... my Web Diary.

So, a little about me: I'm about to wrap up my MS in Accounting at NYU Stern, where I also serve as a Graduate Student Ambassador for the program. This fall I'll be starting as a Commercial Audit Associate at KPMG in Morristown, NJ.

It's been a wild ride getting here - from graduating Summa Cum Laude at Pitt, to interning at both EY and KPMG, to teaching financial accounting to 120 students as a TA. Now I'm wrapping up at Stern and getting ready for the real world.

I decided to build this site because everyone needs a corner of the internet to call their own. And I went with the 90's aesthetic because, honestly, the internet peaked when websites looked like this. Fight me.
